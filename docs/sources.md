# Data Sources — A.R.I.

## News
- **NewsAPI** — global + Indian business news (Economic Times, Mint, NDTV Profit, etc.)
- **ET Markets RSS** — https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/markets/rssfeeds/1977021501.cms  
- **Moneycontrol RSS** — https://www.moneycontrol.com/rss/latestnews.xml  

## Filings (Free Public APIs)
- **BSE India Announcements API**  
  `https://api.bseindia.com/BseIndiaAPI/api/AnnGetData/w?strCat=-1&strPrevDate=<YYYY-MM-DD>&strScrip=<CODE>`
- **NSE India Corporate Announcements API**  
  `https://www.nseindia.com/api/corporate-announcements?index=equities`

## Future Sources
